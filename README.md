# Rubik
A simple text-based rubik cube emulator.

```
                 Y  Y  Y        [SR] Shift right    [SL] Shift left
                 Y  Y  Y        [SU] Shift up       [SD] Shift down
                 Y  Y  Y        [RR] Rotate right   [RL] Rotate left

         ◄ [ 9]  G  G  G  [10] ►
                 G  G  G
         ◄ [11]  G  G  G  [12] ►

                 ▲     ▲
                [5]   [7]

R  R  R   ◄ [1]  W  W  W  [2] ►  O  O  O
R  R  R          W  W  W         O  O  O
R  R  R   ◄ [3]  W  W  W  [4] ►  O  O  O

                [6]   [8]
                 ▼     ▼

                 B  B  B        [U]ndo last move    [S]ave moves
                 B  B  B        [M]ix the cube      [E]xit and save moves
                 B  B  B        [R]eset the cube    [Q]uit without saving
```
